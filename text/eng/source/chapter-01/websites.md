## [Quantum Algorithm Zoo](https://quantumalgorithmzoo.org/)

Quantum Algorithm Zoo is a handful collection of quantum algorithms, written by Dr. Stephen P. Jordan.
Despite the fact that quantum computers promise that they can solve problems faster than classical computers, the speedup can be different by algorithms.
It is important to know which algorithm can be applied to a specific problem to solve on a quantum computer.
A reader can compare the speedup, investigate what kind of problems that a quantum computer can handle, and refer to the original papers to deepen their understanding.
